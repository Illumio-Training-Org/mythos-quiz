Key Concepts to Remember

Mythos changes the speed of the front door — an attacker still has to move laterally to reach anything that matters. That single fact is the foundation of Illumio's entire response.
Illumio's one-sentence position: you can't stop every breach, but you can stop it from spreading.
Vulnerability-agnostic protection means segmentation policy holds regardless of which CVE is used — you don't need to know the exploit for the policy to work.
The 5-step Zero Trust framework: Define the core → Assume breach → Allow only explicit paths → Verify continuously → Isolate fast.
Detection tells you something happened. Containment decides how bad it gets. A Mythos-speed attacker requires both — neither is a substitute for the other.
